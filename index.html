Sequence Numbers

Draw tiles with numbers. The goal is to get ascending series in as many rows and columns as possible. For each box, the number to the right or below must be greater. In total there are 40 tiles with the numbers 1 to 40. From these you get four tiles at a time and must drag them to the board. You get 1 point for each row or column that has an ascending series. You can play multiple times and accumulate more points. Good luck!
